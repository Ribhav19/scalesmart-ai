# Technical Overview

ScaleSmart AI explores integrated AI systems for startup intelligence through:
- transformer-based NLP architectures,
- reinforcement learning optimization,
- retrieval-augmented generation systems,
- explainable AI methodologies,
- and adaptive multi-domain scoring pipelines.

The platform investigates scalable founder intelligence systems tailored for startup ecosystems and venture analytics workflows.

This repository currently serves as a high-level technical and research overview.
